📍 北京名胜 · Famous Beijing Landmarks

亮亮在信里提到了北京的四大名胜：长城是世界上最长的城墙，有两千多年历史！故宫是以前皇帝住的地方，有九千多间房子。

颐和园是北京最大最美的皇家园林，里面有大湖和漂亮的长廊。天安门广场是世界上最大的广场之一！

北京是中国的首都，也是世界著名的历史文化名城。

Liangliang's letter mentions four famous Beijing landmarks: the Great Wall, the world's longest wall with 2,000+ years of history! The Forbidden City was the emperor's palace — with over 9,000 rooms!

The Summer Palace is Beijing's grandest royal garden with a huge lake and beautiful covered walkways. Tiananmen Square is one of the world's largest public squares!

Beijing (北京 = "Northern Capital") is China's capital city and one of the world's great historic cities.
