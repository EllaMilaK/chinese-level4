谜语是一种有趣的文字游戏。猜谜可以让我们动脑筋，学新词。

这课有两个谜语：第一个谜底是电话，第二个谜底是小蜜蜂。

小蜜蜂从小就爱劳动，它们飞来飞去，采花粉，酿花蜜，真了不起！

Riddles are a fun word game! Guessing riddles exercises your brain and teaches new vocabulary.

This lesson has two riddles: the first answer is a telephone, the second is a honey bee.

Honey bees work hard from birth — flying around collecting pollen and making honey. Amazing!
