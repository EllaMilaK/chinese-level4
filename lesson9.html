⭐ 张衡小档案 · Zhang Heng — Who Was He?

张衡（Zhāng Héng）是中国东汉时期著名的天文学家，生活在大约1,900年前（公元78–139年）。他从小就喜欢仰望星空，数星星！

长大后，他绘制了一幅详细的星图，上面记录了2,500颗星星🌟，还命名了320颗。

他还发明了世界上最早的地动仪——专门用来测量地震方向的仪器！

Zhang Heng was a famous astronomer who lived during China's Eastern Han Dynasty, about 1,900 years ago (78–139 CE). As a child, he loved gazing at the night sky and counting stars!

He later created a detailed star map that recorded 2,500 stars 🌟 and named 320 of them.

He also invented the world's first seismograph — a device that could detect the direction of earthquakes!
