🏰 凡尔赛宫的故事 · The Story of Versailles

凡尔赛宫位于法国巴黎以南的小城凡尔赛，曾经是法国的王宫，现在是国家博物馆。

宫里最有名的地方之一是镜厅。打开窗子，园林景色映入镜中，好像人在画中一样。

宫外的园林有人工河、明净的湖、绿色的树和美丽的花，是学习“风景如画”的好例子。

The Palace of Versailles is in the town of Versailles, south of Paris. It was once a French royal palace and is now a national museum.

One famous room is the Hall of Mirrors, where light, paintings, windows, and garden views create a magical scene.

The gardens include canals, lakes, trees, flowers, and carefully designed views.
